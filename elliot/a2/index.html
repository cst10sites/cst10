Introduction

Hello everyone today I will be showing you how to make chocolate chip cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 cup unsalted butter(softened)
  • 2 Large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ unsweatened coco powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½teaspoon salt
  • 1 ⅔ cups white chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Gather all ingrediants and preheat oven to 350 degrees

  2. Beat sugar and butter together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time beating well after each addition then stir in vanilla.
  4. Combine the flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt in a bowl then stir in the creamed mixture.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips
  6. Place spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Bake in oven until cooked around 8 to 10 minutes. Remove from the oven, let cool on the baking sheets for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
